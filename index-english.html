Work experience

Companies and freelance

I have worked as a permanent employee for different companies such as: Inovo Advertising Agency, RG Group (consortium of companies composed of 6 companies) and Network Solutions Control, receiving several awards for my work performance.

As a freelancer I worked for many more like, OPNI creative studio, First meridian mortgage, Dinapix studios, KSA insurance agency, MKT-Network, Picari kitchen, Puntellare, different websites related to graphic design, among many others.

Biography

A little bit about me

Graduate in graphic design with more than 9 years of experience in different branches of design such as web, print, corporate image, packaging, photography, illustrations, among others. Starting as an intern at Inovo Publicidad I developed my skills mainly in the web design and multimedia branch becoming the company’s web master, also working in other branches of advertising design and corporate identity.

Then I worked as a freelance while teaching at "Centro electrónico de idiomas" as a professor of web design and layout, graphic design and substitutions in English classes. While in Stargas I completely leave my comfort zone and I develop mainly in product marketing, making packaging, catalogues, photographs, promotions, POP material, among others, and then become a corporate graphic designer, managing the design of the RG group of companies, introducing me to the design of content for social networks and having other designers under my responsibility.
